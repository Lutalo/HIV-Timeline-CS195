Community Support

San Francisco Aids Foundation

Offers several activities every week. This includes free testing, support groups, counseling, sterile syringes and more.

Shanti PROJECT

Provides counseling to educate about HIV disease, how to navigate the healthcare system and they provide emotional support with drop-in couselling.

Bay Positives

Provides services to help young people (ages 26 and younger) with a weekly drop-in support group and social events. They also provide personal one-on-one help on a case by case basis and more.

AIDS Walk San Francisco

is a yearly fundraiser event. Since 1987, AIDS Walk San Francisco has raised $86 million for HIV programs and services in the Bay Area and has grown into the largest AIDS fundraising even in Northern California.
